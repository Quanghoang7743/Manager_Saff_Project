# Dat van de

Trong boi canh nhu cau lien lac truc tuyen ngay cang cao, cac ung dung nhan tin khong chi can ho tro gui va nhan tin nhan co ban ma con phai dam bao toc do, tinh dong bo, kha nang mo rong va trai nghiem nguoi dung tot tren nhieu thiet bi. Nguoi dung hien nay ky vong mot he thong chat co the cap nhat tin nhan theo thoi gian thuc, hien thi trang thai da gui, da nhan, da doc, dang nhap, tim kiem nguoi dung nhanh va quan ly cuoc tro chuyen mot cach linh hoat.

Tu thuc te do, viec xay dung mot ung dung chat fullstack tro thanh mot bai toan co y nghia ve mat ky thuat va ung dung. He thong khong chi can xu ly giao dien than thien, de su dung, ma con phai ket hop chat che giua frontend, backend, xac thuc nguoi dung, quan ly du lieu va co che realtime de dam bao moi thay doi deu duoc dong bo ngay lap tuc giua cac nguoi dung dang tham gia tro chuyen.

# Bai toan

Bai toan dat ra la thiet ke va xay dung mot ung dung nhan tin thoi gian thuc, trong do nguoi dung co the dang ky, dang nhap, tim kiem ban be, tao cuoc tro chuyen, gui tin nhan, thao tac voi tin nhan va nhan duoc cap nhat theo thoi gian thuc ma khong can tai lai trang. He thong can ho tro ca hoi thoai 1-1 va hoi thoai nhom, dong thoi cung cap cac tinh nang mo rong nhu reaction, typing, read receipt, quan ly participant, mute, pin, archive va cai dat tai khoan.

Ve mat ky thuat, bai toan can giai quyet cac van de sau:

- dong bo du lieu chat theo thoi gian thuc giua nhieu nguoi dung va nhieu thiet bi
- to chuc frontend theo huong de bao tri, de nang cap giao dien va tinh nang
- ket hop co che xac thuc va phan quyen de bao ve cac kenh private
- dam bao giao dien hoat dong tot tren moi truong local va production
- phan tach cau hinh theo moi truong de viec phat trien local khong anh huong toi production

# Muc tieu

Muc tieu cua de tai la xay dung mot ung dung chat co kha nang hoat dong on dinh, de su dung va de mo rong trong thuc te. Cu the:

- Xay dung giao dien nhan tin hien dai bang React trong thu muc `resources/`, tap trung vao trai nghiem chat, tim kiem va cai dat tai khoan.
- Xay dung he thong backend bang Laravel de xu ly xac thuc, quan ly nguoi dung, cuoc tro chuyen, tin nhan, participant va cac API lien quan.
- Trien khai co che realtime de nguoi dung nhan duoc tin nhan moi, typing, reaction, read receipt va cac cap nhat khac ngay lap tuc.
- Ho tro nhieu moi truong van hanh: local su dung Reverb de phat trien va production co the su dung Pusher de dam bao on dinh khi deploy.
- To chuc cau hinh env ro rang, tach biet local va production, giup qua trinh sua giao dien, bo sung tinh nang va deploy duoc thuan loi.
- Tao nen mot nen tang co the tiep tuc nang cap them cac tinh nang nhu quan ly thiet bi, thong bao, profile, setting va cac module mo rong khac trong tuong lai.
